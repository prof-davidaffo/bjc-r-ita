This lab also introduces a new lab page design element: "If There Is Time." This section is included to provide greater support for differentiation:

  • "For You to Do" problems should be completed by all students
  • "If There Is Time" should be attempted by most students
  • "Take It Further" is offered for those students who are moving most quickly.
The goal of this three-tiered approach is to help teachers keep all students moving through the lab pages at the same pace regardless of the pace of individual pairs.
