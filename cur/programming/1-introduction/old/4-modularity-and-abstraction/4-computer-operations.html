The block reports the remainder when the first input is divided by the second. For example, reports 2 because when 17 is divided by 5, the remainder is 2. When one number divides another evenly, the remainder is 0. So, reports 0.

The Boolean expression reports if n is divisible by 2 (meaning n is even) and if n is odd (not divisible by 2 and so the remainder is not 0).
