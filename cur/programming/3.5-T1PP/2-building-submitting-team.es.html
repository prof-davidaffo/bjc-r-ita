Building and Submitting Your Project

Work with your partner to develop your program.

When you have a working project:

  1. Export a summary of your project and save that page as a pdf:
  2. Create a document describing your project...
    1. Describe the purpose of your program and the language that you used (Snap!, of course) to write it. If necessary, say how to use it. (100 words or less)
    2. Describe how your project uses abstraction: (100 words or less)
      • Include an image of part of your program that uses abstraction.
      • Describe how this part of your program uses abstraction.
    3. Describe how your project uses a complex algorithm: (100 words or less)
      • Include an image of the most complicated algorithm in your program.
      • Describe what that algorithm does.
  • Send the summary and description to your teacher.
